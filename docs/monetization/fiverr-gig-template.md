## Gig Description（详情页正文）
Do you have a messy folder of photos/videos/documents with inconsistent filenames?

I will help you **batch rename and organize** your files based on your rules, and deliver a clean output package.

**Key points**
- Works **offline** (privacy-friendly)
- Supports **batch rename** based on patterns
- Optional: deliver as a **ZIP package**
- Optional: provide a **CSV manifest** (old name → new name)

**What I need from you**
- Your files (or a sample subset)
- Your rename rule (examples below)

**Rename rule examples**
- `YYYY-MM-DD_Sequence` (e.g., 2026-02-06_001)
- `ProjectName_ClientName_###`
- Keep extension unchanged (e.g., .jpg, .mp4)

**Deliverables**
- Renamed files (folder structure preserved if requested)
- CSV manifest
- Notes about edge cases (duplicates, invalid characters, etc.)

## FAQ（常见问题）
**Q: Is it really offline?**
A: Yes. The rename process can be done locally. If you share files through Fiverr attachments or cloud links, that’s your chosen delivery method.

**Q: Will you change the file contents?**
A: No. Only filenames (and optionally folder organization) are changed.

**Q: What about duplicate names after renaming?**
A: I will detect conflicts and propose safe suffixes (e.g., _01, _02).
