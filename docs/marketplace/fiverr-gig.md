## Gig Description（可直接粘贴）
If you need to **organize a folder full of files** (photos, documents, downloads) but you **don’t want to upload anything to the cloud**, I can deliver a **100% offline** tool that runs locally in your browser.

What you get:
- A **ready-to-use offline web page** (Lite version) to:
  - scan a folder (via browser folder picker)
  - generate a file inventory
  - optionally rename files with safe rules
  - export inventory as **CSV**
- Clear step-by-step usage instructions
- Optional customization: your preferred naming pattern and rules

Why offline:
- Your files stay on your computer
- No server, no account, no tracking

Demo / download:
- Lite (fully offline): https://peng1233.github.io/offline-file-organizer/lite/
- Repository: https://github.com/peng1233/offline-file-organizer

## FAQ（可选）
Q: Do I need to upload my files?
A: No. The tool runs offline. You keep everything locally.

Q: Will it work without internet?
A: Yes. The Lite version is designed to work fully offline.

Q: Can you guarantee 100% renaming safety?
A: I include safeguards (preview/confirmation). You should still test on a copy first.
